Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Who We Are

HorizantalTabs is a Chrome extension developed and operated by Algomittens LLC. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at algomittens@gmail.com.

2. Single Purpose

HorizantalTabs provides horizontal tab management for Google Chrome. It displays your open tabs in a scrollable horizontal strip so you can search, switch, reorder, and organize tabs efficiently.

3. Data We Collect

3a. Tab Metadata (Local Only)

The extension reads tab information (titles, URLs, favicons, group assignments) using the Chrome Tabs API. This data is used only within the extension popup to display your tabs. It is never sent to any server.

3b. Email Address (Pro Users)

If you upgrade to HorizantalTabs Pro, we store your email address to verify your license. Your email is stored in our database (Supabase) and is used solely for license verification.

3c. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. Stripe handles all payment processing under their own privacy policy.

3d. Thumbnail Cache (Local Only)

For Pro users, the extension captures low-resolution JPEG thumbnails of your active tab for the hover preview feature. These are stored only in the service worker's memory (never on disk, never sent to any server) and are cleared when Chrome closes.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not read page content
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data
  • We do not use cookies

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (database, US-hosted) — stores license records (email + subscription status)
  • Stripe (payment processing) — handles all payment transactions

No other third parties receive any data from this extension.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is shared only with Supabase (license storage) and Stripe (payment processing) as described above.

9. Web Browsing Activity

The extension accesses tab URLs and titles solely to display them in the popup interface. We do not monitor, log, or transmit your browsing activity. No browsing data ever leaves your browser.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data (preferences, sessions, cached license status)
  • Cancelling your subscription marks your license as inactive in our database
  • To delete your data entirely, email algomittens@gmail.com and we will remove your record from Supabase within 30 days

11. Children's Privacy

HorizantalTabs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Security

All communication between the extension and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. License verification uses Supabase's row-level security to ensure users can only read their own license status.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.

14. Chrome Web Store Compliance

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, including the limited use requirements for the Tabs API.
