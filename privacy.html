Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Who We Are

HorizantalTabs is a Chrome extension operated by Algomittens LLC ("we", "us", "our"). You can reach us at support@algomittens.com.

Single Purpose

HorizantalTabs' single purpose is to provide horizontal tab management for Google Chrome. It displays your open tabs in a scrollable horizontal strip so you can search, switch, reorder, group, and organize tabs efficiently. HorizantalTabs does not monitor your general browsing activity. It only reads tab metadata (titles, URLs, favicons) to display them in the extension popup.

1. What Data We Collect

HorizantalTabs collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Tab metadata: Tab titles, URLs, favicons, group assignments, and pinned/active state. This data is read via the Chrome Tabs API and used only within the extension popup to display your tabs. It is never sent to any server.
  • Thumbnail cache: For Pro users, the extension captures low-resolution JPEG thumbnails of your active tab for the hover preview feature. These are stored only in the service worker's memory (never on disk, never sent to any server) and are cleared when Chrome closes.
  • Saved sessions: Tab URLs and titles you choose to save as a session. Stored locally in chrome.storage.local.
  • Preferences: Settings like compact mode, badge toggle, and layout preferences. Stored locally in chrome.storage.local.
  • Email address (Pro users): If you upgrade to HorizantalTabs Pro, we store your email address to verify your license. Your email is stored in our database (Supabase) and is used solely for license verification.
  • Subscription data: If you upgrade to a paid plan, Stripe processes your payment. We store your Stripe customer ID and subscription status — never your card number or payment details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Each type of data we collect serves a specific purpose:

  • Tab metadata is used to display your open tabs in the horizontal strip, enable search, and show tab group labels and colors.
  • Thumbnails are used to show a preview when you hover over a tab in the strip (Pro only).
  • Saved sessions are used to let you restore a group of tabs you previously saved.
  • Preferences are used to remember your layout and display settings between sessions.
  • Email address is used to verify your Pro license status.
  • Subscription data is used to determine whether Pro features (thumbnails, unlimited sessions) are active for your account.

3. How We Handle Your Data

We take the following measures to handle your data securely:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the extension and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). No user data is ever sent over unencrypted HTTP connections.
  • Local-first architecture: Tab metadata, thumbnails, saved sessions, and preferences are stored entirely on your device in chrome.storage.local and service worker memory. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Minimal server-side data: Our servers store only license verification data (email and subscription status). Tab data and preferences stay on your device.
  • Row-level security: License verification uses Supabase's row-level security to ensure users can only read their own license status.
  • No analytics or tracking: HorizantalTabs does not include any analytics libraries, telemetry, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting code.

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure. While we use reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Where Data Is Stored

  • On your device: Tab metadata, thumbnails, saved sessions, preferences, and cached license status are stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local and service worker memory.
  • Supabase (US-hosted): Email address and subscription status are stored in our Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database.
  • Stripe: Payment method details and billing history are stored by Stripe. We do not have access to your full card number.

5. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is shared only with the following service providers, solely to operate HorizantalTabs:

  • Supabase — license verification and subscription management. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Stripe Privacy Policy.

No other third parties receive any data from this extension.

7. Web Browsing Activity

HorizantalTabs does not collect or use your web browsing activity except as necessary to provide its single purpose of horizontal tab management. Any browsing-related information handled by HorizantalTabs is limited to tab titles and URLs, which are used only to display your tabs in the extension popup. HorizantalTabs does not collect your browsing history, track which websites you visit, or read page content. Tab metadata is stored only in the extension's local memory and is not transmitted to our servers.

8. What We Don't Collect

  • Browsing history or activity beyond tab titles and URLs displayed in the popup
  • Page content, cookies, form data, or login credentials
  • Personal files, device identifiers, or hardware information
  • Analytics, telemetry, advertising IDs, or tracking pixels
  • Full credit card numbers (payment details are handled entirely by Stripe)

9. Data Retention

  • Local data (tab metadata, thumbnails, saved sessions, preferences) is retained on your device until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.
  • License data (email, subscription status) is retained on our servers until you cancel your subscription or request deletion.
  • Payment records are retained by Stripe per their retention policy.

10. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Local data: Uninstall the extension or clear your browser's extension data. This removes all preferences, saved sessions, and cached license status.
  • Saved sessions: Delete individual sessions from within the extension.
  • Server-side data: Cancel your Pro subscription, then email support@algomittens.com to request complete deletion of your license record from our database. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the extension and requesting data deletion
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email support@algomittens.com.

12. Children's Privacy

HorizantalTabs is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

13. Cookies

HorizantalTabs does not use cookies. License status is stored in chrome.storage.local, which is accessible only to the extension.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of HorizantalTabs after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Chrome Web Store User Data Policy Compliance

HorizantalTabs' use of user data adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • HorizantalTabs only uses data in ways that are necessary to provide and improve the extension's single purpose: horizontal tab management, including displaying, searching, reordering, and organizing your open tabs.
  • HorizantalTabs does not transfer user data to third parties except as necessary to provide the service (as described in Section 5), to comply with applicable laws, or to protect against malware, spam, phishing, or other fraud or abuse.
  • HorizantalTabs does not use or transfer user data for personalized advertisements.
  • HorizantalTabs does not sell user data to third parties, data brokers, or information resellers.
  • HorizantalTabs does not use or transfer user data to determine credit-worthiness or for lending purposes.
  • No human reads your user data unless (a) you give explicit consent for a specific support request, (b) it is necessary for security purposes such as investigating abuse, or (c) it is required to comply with applicable law.

16. Financial & Authentication Data

HorizantalTabs does not publicly disclose any financial or payment information. Credit card details are handled exclusively by Stripe and are never accessible to us. License verification tokens stored in chrome.storage.local are never publicly disclosed, logged in plaintext, or exposed to third parties.
