How it works

Open the popup, see every tab at a glance, click to switch.

Open the popup

Click the icon or press Alt+A. Every tab appears as a horizontal card.

Find & switch

Start typing to search. Click any tab to switch. Arrow keys to navigate.

Organize

Drag to reorder. Right-click for options. Close tabs with a single click.
