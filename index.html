What you get

Horizontal strip All your tabs in a row. Scroll with your mouse wheel. Free
Search Just type. Filters by title and URL instantly. Free
Drag to reorder Grab a tab, drop it where you want it. Free
Tab groups Color-coded groups show inline. Click to collapse. Free
Keyboard nav Arrow keys to browse, Enter to switch, / to search, Esc to close. Free
Tab count badge See how many tabs you have. Toggle it on or off. Free
Light / Dark theme Toggle between light, dark, or auto (follow system). Free
Thumbnails Hover a tab to see a screenshot of the page. Pro
Sessions Save all your tabs, close them, restore them later. Pro
Undo close Ctrl+Z to bring back the tab you just closed. Pro
Multi-window See tabs from all windows in one view. Pro
Compact mode Favicon-only view when you have 50+ tabs. Pro
Multi-row layout Display tabs in 2 or 3 rows for a grid view. Pro
Export URLs Copy every open tab URL to your clipboard. Pro
